About Me

Hello! Thanks for checking out my personal website! I am always continually improving it, so feel free to give feedback!

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I'm deeply interested in full-stack web development, as well as software engineering and data analytics, but I'm also looking into expanding my knowledge in other areas of technology as well.

I'm passionate about bridging the diversity gap in the technology industry, as well as increasing visibility for the Asian American LGBTQ population. I am also deeply interested in software for social good.

In my (little-to-none) spare time, you can find me hanging out in the WCS office at UIUC, drafting my next post on Medium, trying to relive my figure skating days at the U of I ice rink and/or singing at the top of my lungs to my 4-hour-long main playlist on Spotify.
